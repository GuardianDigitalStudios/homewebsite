About Us

Built on creativity,
driven by purpose

Guardian Digital Studios, L.L.C. was founded in Gaithersburg, Maryland as a creative outlet with a dual mission: build innovative software solutions that tackle everyday problems at a price point that respects users, and develop engaging video games that started as ideas between friends.

We believe that great apps shouldn't require great expense. The leading tools in the market often charge a premium for features that should be accessible to everyone. Our approach is different — we build lean, focused products that deliver genuine value without unnecessary bloat or inflated pricing.

On the gaming side, we pursue the projects that excite us. Every game we develop starts from a spark of genuine enthusiasm — an idea pitched over dinner, a mechanic that keeps us up at night, a story we need to tell. That passion translates directly into the player experience.
