Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Guardian Digital Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Prowess mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you choose to provide, such as your display name and profile picture.

Fitness Data

We collect workout data that you log in the app, including:

  • Exercises performed
  • Sets, reps, and weights
  • Workout dates and durations
  • Personal records
  • Fitness goals

Health App Data

With your permission, we may access data from Apple Health or Google Health Connect, including:

  • Weight measurements
  • Step counts
  • Sleep data
  • Workout data from other apps

This data is used solely to provide you with a comprehensive view of your fitness progress within the app.

Nutrition Data

If you use diet tracking features, we collect meal and nutrition information you log. We use the Open Food Facts database for food information, which is a public database.

Device Information

We may collect device information such as your device type and operating system version to ensure app compatibility and improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Track your fitness progress and personal records
  • Enable social features (if you choose to share)
  • Improve and optimize the app
  • Communicate with you about your account

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our cloud servers powered by Supabase. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing

Social Features

If you enable sharing features, other users may see:

  • Your workouts (if enabled)
  • Your personal records (if enabled)
  • Your goals (if enabled)

You control what you share through the privacy settings in the app.

Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our app (e.g., cloud hosting)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

Health Data

Health data accessed from Apple Health or Google Health Connect is treated with special care:

  • Health data is only accessed with your explicit permission
  • We never sell or share your health data with third parties
  • You can disconnect health app integration at any time in Settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for health app access
  • Control your sharing settings

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
